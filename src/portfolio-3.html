Building ada-tracer to help the Cardano Community

Last Updated 3/13/2022

Ada Tracer is a site that helps to improve the visibility into the cardano blockchain. The site has three main phases, V1, 2 and 3.

V1 is already live and allows a user to enter a wallet address or a staking address and see there reward history displayed as a graph and in a table. The table format is then easy to copy and paste over to track cost basis and other positions.

V2 will focus on bluechip NFT collections and will allow users to track ownership and sale prices.

V3 will live up to the name of the site the most with the focus being on ada and other native assets. Tracing their flow from wallet to wallet through the blockchain.
